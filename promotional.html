Context
Benefits:
-Boost your self-esteem and confidence
-Gain more friends
-Better communication
-This type of skills can give you opportunities on having someone to rely on
-Initiate conversations easily
-Learn what to do and what not to do when talking to different types of people.
Goal
Our goal, precisely, is to help all people communicate more easily so they can gain more friends or be able to converse with random people with ease, as well as increase self-esteem, both of which the audience can benefit from.

People tend to lack socialization skills these days especially since social media is a thing now and people can communicate with each other without meeting in person. Thus, we aim to improve how people socialize in real life.
Workshop
Our workshop will include warmup activities such as sharing stories and 4Pics1Word! It will also include education on different ways to socialize, different personality types, and social etiquette.
